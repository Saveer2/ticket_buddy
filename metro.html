Metro Booking Steps

1. Open app

Tap on the metro app icon on your phone to get started.

2. Select book ticket

From the app's home screen, choose the 'Book New Ticket' or 'Plan Journey' option.

3. Choose destination

Select your starting station (or 'Current Station') and then choose your destination station from the list or map.

4. Confirm your details

Review your journey details, including the route, number of tickets, and total fare.

5. Proceed to pay

Tap 'Proceed to Pay' and select your preferred payment method (UPI, card, wallet) to complete the booking.
