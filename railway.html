Railway Booking Steps

1. Open app

Launch the railway booking app from your phone's main screen.

2. Select train option

On the homepage, select the 'Book Train Tickets' or 'Plan My Journey' option.

3. Choose your starting and ending location

Enter your departure station in the 'From' field and your arrival station in the 'To' field. Select your travel date.

4. Choose train, tickets, and pay

Browse the list of available trains. Select a train with available seats, choose your class, add passenger details, and then proceed to payment.
