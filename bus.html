Bus Booking Steps

1. Open app

Tap on the bus booking application icon on your phone's home screen to launch it.

2. Select bus ticket

From the main menu, choose the 'Book Bus Tickets' option to begin.

3. Add your starting and ending point

Enter your departure city in the 'From' field and your destination in the 'To' field.

4. Now pay for your ticket

Choose your preferred payment method and complete the transaction to confirm your booking.
